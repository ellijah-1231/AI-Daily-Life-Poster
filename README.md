# Artificial Intelligence in Daily Life – Poster Design

## Overview
This project is a poster design that highlights the growing influence of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in our daily lives. Using a futuristic visual style, the poster illustrates how AI is transforming key sectors such as education, healthcare, industry, and smart living.

## Design Theme
- Futuristic and technology-inspired
- Neon blue and purple color palette
- Modern typography and AI visuals
- Clean and engaging layout

## Key Areas Highlighted
### Education
AI helps personalize learning and supports students through intelligent educational tools.

### Healthcare
AI assists in medical diagnosis, patient care, and healthcare management.

### Industry
AI enhances productivity, automation, and operational efficiency.

### Daily Life
AI powers smart devices, virtual assistants, and personalized digital experiences.

## Tools Used
- Canva

## Purpose
The purpose of this poster is to visually communicate the importance of Artificial Intelligence and its impact on modern society in a simple and engaging manner.

## Internship Project
This poster was created as part of a Graphic Design Internship to demonstrate skills in visual communication, layout design, and creative design.
